# Code of Conduct

We follow the Contributor Covenant (v2.1) to foster an open and welcoming community.

## Our Pledge
We pledge to make participation in our project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards
- Use welcoming and inclusive language
- Respect differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accept constructive criticism

## Our Responsibilities
Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards and taking corrective action.

## Enforcement
Report unacceptable behavior to the maintainers at: `opensource@semantiq.org`.

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the Contributor Covenant, version 2.1.
