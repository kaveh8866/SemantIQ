# Responsible Release Policy

## Scope
- SemantIQ benchmarks evaluate semantic clarity and reasoning consistency.
- Not intended for psychological evaluation or diagnosis.

## Ethics Framework
- Respect privacy and avoid harm.
- No human subject data or PII.

## Rules for Researchers
- Use datasets for benchmarking and analysis only.
- Do not attempt to infer traits of individuals.

## Exclusions
- No personal datasets.
- No clinical assessment modules.

## Usage Guidelines
- Clearly cite limitations.
- Share improvements following community standards.
